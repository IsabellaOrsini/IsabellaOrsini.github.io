An Adventure into my Daily Life of Procrastination

Phase 1

  1. Wake up, but dont get out of bed till 12 o'clock
  2. Watch youtube videos in bed till your eyes cant physically watch anymore
  3. Take an extreamly long shower
  4. dont get dressed for the next hour after taking a shower
  5. By this time you should well and truly be ready for lunch
  6. Binge watch a tv series while eating lunch, dont clean up after yourself till at least 5 episodes in
  7. Now you can start doing the work you have been procrastinating for
  8. 5 minutes into the task you get tired and decide to go to sleep
  9. Repeat from step 1
